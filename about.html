UNO Café – Our Story

UNO Café was born from simple moments — spontaneous coffee nights with family and friends while camping. Those late-night talks over a warm cup of coffee reminded us how powerful connection can be. That feeling of comfort and togetherness inspired us to build a place where everyone could share the same experience.

Before opening the café in 2020, we started by selling bento boxes to companies, preparing meals for morning, afternoon, and dinner. That humble beginning taught us hard work, consistency, and the importance of serving with heart — values that continue to shape UNO Café today.

We became known for our handcrafted drinks like the Spanish Latte and Caramel Latte, along with non-coffee favorites such as Strawberry Milk & Matcha. What makes customers keep coming back is our commitment to being affordable. We believe everyone should enjoy great coffee in a cozy, welcoming space without worrying about high prices.

At UNO Café, every cup tells a story — one of warmth, simplicity, and connection. It’s not just coffee; it’s comfort shared, one sip at a time.
